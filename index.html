The Reality

What buyers see and discount for

Every PE firm and strategic acquirer runs the same playbook. They identify operational gaps, discount your price, then capture that value after close.

Key-Person Dependency

Critical knowledge lives in your head. Customer relationships are yours personally. Buyers see a business that can't run without you.

Typical discount: 0.5x – 1.5x EBITDA

Limited Visibility

No dashboards, inconsistent reporting, financials that require explanation. Buyers can't underwrite what they can't see.

Typical discount: 0.25x – 0.5x EBITDA

Legacy Systems

Outdated ERP, disconnected tools, manual workarounds. Buyers see integration costs and operational risk.

Typical discount: 0.25x – 0.75x EBITDA

Undocumented Operations

No SOPs, tribal knowledge, processes that "just work." Buyers see transition risk and hidden fragility.

Typical discount: 0.5x – 1x EBITDA
Combined, these gaps cost sellers 1–3x EBITDA in exit value.
